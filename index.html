How To Make Cookies

Who wouldn't want to enjoy cookies on any day of the week? When you get home from school, all someone is thinking about is food and what to eat. Cookies are the easiest and fun way to enjoy an afternoon snack for not only you but for your family members too. I bet your other family members will be so happy and like you better, after having a delicious warm cookie that melts in your mouth.

    Step 1: Gather All Ingredients
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • One ounce bag semisweet chocolate chips
    Step 2: Preheat Oven to 350 degrees F! While the oven is heating...
  • Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.

Step 3: Bake for about 10-15 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned. Eat up! And! Enjoy!
